## Notes on illustration strategy

- All figures use the **[FIGURE: ...]** notation, which aligns with Attenborough × Feynman style (minimal caption, lets image carry weight)
- Figures are placed at natural breaks in argument, not scattered randomly
- Two major figures are embedded mechanism figures (frost line, aggregation); two are evidence figures (craters, exoplanet disk)
- No decorative images; each figure serves to make a claim visible or clarify a process
